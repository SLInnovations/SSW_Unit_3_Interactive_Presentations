Pick the Best Revision
Which version uses the most sentence variety skills?
Original: "We went to the museum. We saw dinosaur bones. We saw a space exhibit. We ate lunch. We went home."
A) "We went to the museum and saw dinosaur bones and a space exhibit and ate lunch and went home." — just one long sentence
B) "We went to the museum. We saw dinosaur bones and a space exhibit. We ate lunch. Then we went home." — some combining, but still repetitive beginnings
C) "Our class visited the museum, and the dinosaur exhibit took our breath away. Enormous bones towered over us. After exploring the space exhibit, we ate lunch in the courtyard, then headed home. What an incredible day." — varied types, beginnings, lengths, combining
